Meena (මීනා)

Meena (මීනා), written by A. Simon de Silva in 1905, is widely recognized as the first Sinhala novel. The story follows Meena, a young woman navigating adventures, challenges, and complex relationships, reflecting the social and emotional life of the time. This landmark novel holds an important place in Sinhala literature and paved the way for modern Sinhala fiction.

About The Author

A. Saiman De Silva (ඒ. සයිමන් ද සිල්වා) is a notable author in the Sri Lankan literary scene, known for his contributions to the field of literature. His works are available for purchase through various online bookstores, ensuring that readers can access his writings easily.
